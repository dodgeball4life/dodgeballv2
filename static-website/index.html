Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know about the dodgeball fun.

What is Dodgeball?+
Dodgeball is a fast-paced, team-based sport where you dodge, throw, and catch soft foam balls to eliminate the other team.
What is the average age of the players?+
Our players range from 4 to 50 years old. We welcome youth, students, professionals, and everyone in between.
When and where do you play?+
We play on Saturday morning / afternoons. In winter we’re indoors, and if the weather is good, we play outside.
How much does it cost?+
We offer day passes and memberships. Prices are kept accessible, and funds are available if needed.
Do you offer any financial support?+
Yes! You can apply for support through the Volwassenenfonds (for adults) or Jeugdfonds (for youth).
